About

VoteSC represents a coalition of students and groups on campus dedicated to registering USC to vote and promoting civic engagement.




Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get involved?

(1) You must attend the Unruh Open House in SOS B15 on January 17, 2018 at 5:30 PM. If you cannot attend this event, please reach out to our executive board at unruhassociates@gmail.com and we can accommodate your application.
(2) The written application opens January 18, 2018 and is due January 23, 2018 at 5:00 PM.
(3) An individual interview will be conducted sometime between January 24-25, 2018.

Applicants will be notified of their application status after the written application and interview rounds. Shortly after the interviews we will welcome our new members!

How long is the application process?

The application process takes about 7 days from the written application due date on January 23, 2018 to final decisions.
